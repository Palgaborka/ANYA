The Artist

Katalin

Every ANYA design begins as an original work by Katalin—developed slowly, with a painter’s attention to balance, negative space, and the way a room holds light.

Her practice lives between quiet modernism and organic gesture: forms that feel collected over time rather than “made for a trend.”

For designers, the goal is simple—wallcoverings that elevate a space with presence, not noise.
