ANYA

Artist-Created Wallcoverings

Original artwork transformed into exclusive wallcoverings for interior designers who value authenticity over mass production.

ANYA means “mother” in Hungarian—a tribute to the women who taught us to see beauty in simplicity.

Studio to Wall

Each design begins in our studio—abstract, botanical, minimalist compositions created for high-end residential spaces that deserve to feel collected, not decorated.

Made to Live With

We design for rooms people actually use—morning light, dinner conversations, quiet corners. The work is meant to hold presence without demanding attention.

The Collection

Some pieces are airy and architectural; others lean botanical or painterly. Together they’re unified by restraint, texture, and a preference for timeless composition.

Scale & Quiet Drama

Wallcoverings are at their best when they read as atmosphere first, artwork second—creating depth, warmth, and an effortless sense of intention.

Details Matter

We obsess over balance—negative space, tonal contrast, and how a line feels from across the room. It’s the difference between “pretty” and truly livable luxury.

For Designers

If you’re specifying for a project, we’ll help you choose the right mood and scale—and make sure the final installation feels seamless, intentional, and elevated.
